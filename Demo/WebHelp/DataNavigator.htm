Data Navigator

The Navigator contains several buttons to Browse and Edit Data:

Movement buttons:

  • First Record
  • Prior Record
  • Next Record
  • Last Record

Editing buttons:

  • Insert Record
  • Delete Record
  • Edit Record
  • Confirm/Save Data
  • Cancel Editing

At the bottom-left of the Tab, you can change the image associated to the fish.

At the right of the panel you can enter a full description.

At the top-right, you can use the “Data Navigator” to move the data cursor and edit table.

Click to the “Edit Data” Tab to Edit the data.

The Navigator can be used into “Browse Data” Tab or in the “Edit Data” Tab
